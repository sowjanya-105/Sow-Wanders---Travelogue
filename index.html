My Wanderlust Journey

I don't know how, when or from where did I catch a sudden impulse towards travel. I'm not sure if I could make a career out of it, but I strongly see it as my passion because it not just excites me, but I clearly do see the power that comes when I'm into it. I just can't explain that feel, that impulse. It is just beyond words. I haven't travelled much in the recent times, but there are a lot of places in my so called bucket list. I would definitely explore more often, travel to uncharted places and experience all the adventures. It will surely be on my priority list no matter what happens. But yes, I have to wait for it at least for a couple of years ahead to stand on my own feet in order to get out of my comfort zone and travel with no regrets.
Though I have travelled a lot of places with my parents and grandma during my childhood, but I had never felt this impulse before. I have now started to feel the beauty in every little thing around me, the trees, the clouds, those little creatures and everything the God has given us in the form of nature.


Destinations

Andaman Islands

The Andaman & Nicobar Islands are unique. A paragon of beauty, these islands present a landscape of scenic and picturesque extravaganza, shimmering like emeralds in the Bay of Bengal.

Kashmir

Without any doubt, Kashmir is one of the prettiest states of India! Deck up shikaras, beautiful locations, scenic valleys and the smiling face of Kashmiri people are the things every traveler must experience.

Ooty

One of the famous tourist places for the honeymooners; the hill station about which we heard for the umpteenth time since childhood. This place is truly mesmerizing.

Ancient Temples in TN

Tamil Nadu is known for its beautiful temples that are shining examples of the cultural heritage of India along with some of the finest architectural creations that take you back to the history.

Delhi

Started with Delhi heritage walk and Rickshaw tour. It was the perfect introduction of Old Delhi. I enjoyed everything. The sights sounds and tastes have brought this part of Delhi alive for us.

Pondicherry

Wanderlust travelers consider Pondicherry as one of their top travel goals especially in TN. It is also called “The Europe of India”. It has a French colonial legacy that exudes its rich heritage.

Cochin

Kochi is also known as Cochin is a vivacious city located on the south-west coast of the Indian peninsula. Cochin is also referring to as the Gateway to Kerala. The outstanding port city of Cochin is a tourist’s heaven.
